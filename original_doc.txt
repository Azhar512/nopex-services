 1. Purpose of This Document
This document provides complete, ready-to-implement website content for Nopex Services, positioned as an established, multi-disciplinary engineering design firm with national reach — offering Civil, Structural, Architectural, MEP, and Industrial engineering design services, with BIM/Revit modeling as one capability within that broader stack.
The scope and tone here are calibrated to an established national firm — not a small local shop, but also not a 50,000-employee global multinational. Claims of global scale, decades-old landmark projects, or work with government megaprojects are deliberately left out, since those wouldn't be credible or honest for Nopex to claim.
Two operating-model facts shape the language throughout this document, and should not be changed without checking with the business owner first:
•	Stamped/sealed deliverables are provided through a licensed Professional Engineer (PE) partner network covering all 50 US states — not by in-house Nopex engineers. Content below says “in partnership with licensed PEs” rather than “our licensed engineers,” intentionally.
•	Nopex does not perform on-site/field services (surveying, on-site inspection, laser scan capture). Content below frames the offering as design, engineering, and BIM/coordination work, delivered remotely.
